Embyá

Embyá is a Brazilian landscape studio practicing ecosystem-based design — through a sensitive perspective, it seeks a reconciliation between human occupation and natural processes.

Founded by Duarte Vaz, the team gathers 20+ architects, biologists and agronomists working across parks, gardens, historic restoration and environmental studies. Projects grow from analysis and design to cultivation in the studio's own 10,000 m² organic nursery, implementation and maintenance.
